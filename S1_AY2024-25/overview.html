ST5207: Nonparametric Regression (Module Outline)

  1. Lecturer: Qingwei Liu, Email: liu_qw@nus.edu.sg
  2. Module description: The basic idea of nonparametric inference is to use data to infer an unknown quantity with as few assumptions as possible. This course is designed to introduce students to smoothing methods in nonparametric regressions. R will be used to demonstrate and implement the techniques.
  3. Recommended textbooks:
    1. Multivariate density estimation: theory, practice, and visualization, by Scott, D.~W. (2015)., second edition.
    2. Nonparametric and semiparametric models, by Wolfgang Härdle, Axel Werwatz, Marlene Müller, and Stefan Sperlich. (2004).

    Both books can be downloaded from NUS Library.

  4. Topics covered:
    1. Histogram
    2. Kernel Density Estimation
    3. Multivariate Kernel Density Estimation
    4. General Linear Nonparametric Estimation
    5. Other Smoothing methods
